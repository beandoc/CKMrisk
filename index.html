Evidence base — key trials & equations

CKD-EPI 2021
eGFR estimation · Inker LA et al., NEJM 2021;385:1737. Race-free CKD-EPI 2021 equation (age, sex, serum creatinine). Replaced 2009 equation in international guidelines.
KFRE
Kidney Failure Risk Equation · Tangri N et al., JAMA 2016;315:164. 4-variable model. Validated in CKD G3–G5. 2-yr base survival 0.9832; 5-yr 0.9365. Risk thresholds: 5-yr <5% Low · 5–14% Moderate · 15–29% High · ≥30% Very High (KDIGO 2022).
PREVENT
AHA PREVENT Equations · Khan SS et al., Circulation 2024;149:430. Sex-specific logistic models. Validated ages 30–79. Outcome: CHD + stroke + HF + AF (broader than PCE). Incorporates eGFR as independent predictor. CVD thresholds: <5% Low · 5–7.5% Borderline · 7.5–20% Intermediate · ≥20% High.
CREDENCE
Canagliflozin + DKD · Perkovic V et al., NEJM 2019;380:2295. Primary kidney endpoint RRR 30%. eGFR decline ~1.5 mL/yr less vs placebo. CV death/HF −31%.
EMPA-KIDNEY
Empagliflozin in broad CKD · EMPA-KIDNEY Collaborative Group, NEJM 2023;388:117. Broad CKD (eGFR 20–44 or ≥45 + UACR ≥200), with and without T2D. Kidney progression/CV death RRR 28%. eGFR decline ~1.37 mL/yr less.
FIDELIO-DKD
Finerenone in T2D + CKD · Bakris GL et al., NEJM 2020;383:2219. Kidney endpoint RRR 18%; CV endpoint RRR 14%. DM + albuminuric CKD only.
FIGARO-DKD
Finerenone — CV-primary · Pitt B et al., NEJM 2021;385:2252. CV endpoint RRR 13%; kidney secondary endpoint RRR 21%. Combined FIDELITY analysis confirms consistent class effect.
FLOW
Semaglutide in T2D + CKD · Perkovic V et al., NEJM 2024;391:1– . Primary kidney endpoint RRR 24%. eGFR slope attenuation ~1.16 mL/min/yr. MACE −18%. All-cause death −20%. On top of standard-of-care including SGLT2i.
RENAAL · IDNT
ACEi/ARB in DKD · Brenner BM (RENAAL) NEJM 2001; Lewis JB (IDNT) NEJM 2001. ARBs reduce ESKD risk ~16–20%, proteinuria ~35%. Standard of care in DKD.
